# Privacy Policy for Recurring Expense Tracker

**Last Updated: March 7, 2026**

I am the developer of **Recurring Expense Tracker**. I built this app with a "privacy-first" mindset. This policy explains how I handle your information—or more accurately, why I don’t handle it at all.

---

### 1. No Data Collection or Transmission
**I do not collect, use, or share any of your personal data.** * **No Personal Info:** I do not ask for, and have no access to, your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.
* **No Financial Tracking:** Any expenses, categories, or schedules you enter into the app are stored only on your device. I have no way of seeing what you spend or how you use the app.
* **No Server Communication:** The app does not send any data to external servers or cloud services. It works entirely offline.

### 2. Local Data Storage
All data you create within the app stays on your phone.

* **Storage:** Data is kept in the app's local database on your device.
* **Security:** Because I don't have access to your device, I recommend using your phone’s built-in security features (PIN, fingerprint, or face unlock) to keep your data safe.
* **Deletion:** If you uninstall the app, all your data is permanently deleted from your device. Since I do not maintain any backups, I cannot recover this data for you once it is deleted.

### 3. Third-Party Services
While the app itself does not collect data, the platform you use to download it might.

* **Google Play Store:** If you installed this application from Google Play, then Google Play does collect some personal data (such as your Google Account info and app download history). Please see the privacy policy for Google Play Services here: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* **Other App Stores:** If you downloaded this app from a different store, please refer to that specific store's privacy policy regarding your account and download history.
* **GitHub:** If you are interacting with the source code on the official GitHub repository, GitHub’s own privacy practices apply to your account activity there.

### 4. Global Privacy Compliance
Because I do not collect, process, or store any personal data, this app is inherently compliant with major global privacy laws, including:

* **GDPR (EU):** No "Personal Data" is processed.
* **CCPA/CPRA (USA):** No personal information is sold or shared.
* **LGPD (Brazil) & PIPEDA (Canada):** The app's zero-collection model meets the requirements of these frameworks.

### 5. Children’s Privacy
I do not collect any information from anyone, including children. This app is safe for all ages as it does not track or store any user information.

### 6. Changes to This Policy
I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since I don’t have your contact information, I cannot notify you of changes personally. I recommend checking this policy periodically within the app or on the official GitHub repository.

### 7. Contact
If you have any questions about this policy or the app’s privacy practices, you can reach out via the official GitHub repository: [https://github.com/DennisBauer/RecurringExpenseTracker](https://github.com/DennisBauer/RecurringExpenseTracker)
